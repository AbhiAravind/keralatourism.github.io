Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of Kerala. Known for its rich cultural heritage and beautiful beaches, it is a popular destination for tourists. The city offers a blend of ancient temples, beautiful beaches and scenic backwaters. Some of the popular tourist attractions include the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam Beach, Varkala Beach, and the Napier Museum. Visitors can also explore the nearby Agastyakoodam, the highest peak in the Western Ghats and a popular trekking destination.

Places to visit

Places to visit in Thiruvananthapuram.

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

This ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is known for its intricate carvings and architectural style
Location

Kovalam Beach

This picturesque beach is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing. It is also known for its lighthouse and several restaurants and shops nearby.

Napier Museum

This museum is known for its collection of historical artifacts and artworks, including a collection of bronze sculptures and traditional South Indian musical instruments.

Varkala beach

This art gallery features a collection of traditional Indian and Rajput paintings, as well as works by European artists

Kanakakkunnu Palace

This palace is a popular tourist destination for its beautiful gardens and the views of the city. It also features an open-air theater and an indoor stadium.
